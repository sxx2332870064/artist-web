About Me

"WHY HUMANS?"

Bridging Humanity and Digital Realms

I'm a digital artist passionate about merging humanity with digital media. My portfolio features digital paintings, illustrations, animations, video editing, and coding projects invoving software operations and hardware installations. I explore the interplay between organic bodies and the limitless potential of digital art, aiming to provoke thought and inspire contemplation within the virtual realm.

Exploring Futures and Multiverses

My art explores apocalyptic scenarios, science-fiction innovations, human-robot dynamics, and humanized animals to envision possible futures given current social issues. I aim to provoke reflection and dialogue about the world we're creating and the paths we're on. Welcome to my vision of tomorrow.

Unveiling the Essence of AI

I delve into the world of AI-generated content and the construction of AI setups. My art brings AI to life through robotic installations and animated stories. Join me in exploring the fusion of technology and creativity in the evolving human-AI relationship.

Welcome to my digital canvas, where art converges with technology to explore the ever-evolving interplay of humanity, artificial intelligence, and the boundless possibilities of the digital realm.

PORTFOLIO (videos)

hello_world_dreams

"hello_world_dreams" explores dream-core aesthetics within the context of artificial intelligence. It portrays an imagined world within the AI's mind, marked by glitches that highlight its limitations. The narrative conveys societal constraints on individuality, points out the current issues of objectifying women, amd calls awarenesses to the rapid developments on technologies & AI, creating a dark atmosphere. The audio, a mix of echoing rhythms and sci-fi effects, enhances immersion. The video combines 3D animations, doll recordings, images, and soundscapes to offer an immersive, interactive experience in a dark room where viewers lie down.

Recoil

For my final project "Recoil," I aim to explore the intricate relationship between humans, wildlife, and the natural world. Through a blend of online footage, 2D and 3D animation, I depict the demise of a half-human, half-animal character and conclude with a profound eye-closing shot layered over filtered footage. This piece delves into hypothetical realities, evoking empathy and underscoring the potential desperation resulting from the neglect of harmony between humanity and nature. Combining various natural scene footages, hand-drawn frame-by-frame animation, and 3D character animation, "Recoil" experiments with cross-dimensional art, seeking to push the boundaries of artistic and conceptual expression.

Arcane Orb

I crafted an interactive light ball using Arduino Nano, an MPU6050 accelerometer, Neopixel light strips, and 3D printed models. This captivating artwork invites the audience to manipulate the light ball's display by changing its orientation and enjoying a sparkling effect when shaken. Through this engaging experience, I aim to underscore the significance of new media robotics. The programming involves setting up the accelerometer, defining output ranges, and synchronizing different light sequences with the artwork's orientation. Thanks to its compact size and portability, "Arcane Orb" can be easily enjoyed by audiences in various settings.

Phone nomena

I aim to explore visual illusions in moving images while addressing our excessive reliance on technology, particularly smartphones, which continuously imprint shapes like rectangles and four-pointed stars in our minds. Through scenes of shape-shifting and fading reminiscent of electronic device markings, I seek to depict the consequences of overuse. Drawing inspiration from James Clar's impactful light installations, I employ 2D animations to create an abstract, science-fiction atmosphere. My work features frame-by-frame animation blended with online footage, sound effects, and visual effects to convey this concept effectively.
